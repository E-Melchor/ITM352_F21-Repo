ABOUT THE CAFE

Tea Cafe was founded in 2021 by Helen Lin when she first had to build her first e-commerce website. The built e-commerce website, meant as a assignment had turned into an actual business after the end of the semester. The startup of this cafe was meant not to only bring together those who loves tea but also those who need to take time and relax after doing their ITM352 assignments.

In addition to doing school work, Ms. Lin wants more people to come together and enjoy the beauty of tea flavors and be immersed in the peace that each tea flavor brings along.

"Use products from nature for what it's worth - but never too early, nor too late." Fresh is the new sweet.

Shidler student: Helen Lin

Opening hours: Mon-Fri from 9am to 10pm.

Address: 814 Aloha street, 2000, HI
